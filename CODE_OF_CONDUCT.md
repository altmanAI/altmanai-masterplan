# Code of Conduct

AltmanAI is committed to building human-first AI. We expect participants to treat each other with dignity and respect.

## Our standards
We will:
- Be respectful and constructive
- Assume good intent and focus on solutions
- Protect privacy and safety
- Be honest about uncertainty and limitations

We will not:
- Harass, demean, or discriminate
- Encourage harm, violence, or illegal activity
- Share private information without consent
- Misrepresent work, results, or provenance

## Enforcement
Maintainers may remove, edit, or reject contributions that violate this code of conduct. Repeated or severe violations may result in bans.

## Reporting
If you experience or witness a violation, email: security@altmanai.tech (or open a private security report per `SECURITY.md`).
